# 🚀 CODEWARZ 2026 – 6 Hour Hackathon

Welcome to the official repository for **CODEWARZ 2026**, a 6-hour software hackathon organized by the Department of ECE in association with IEEE Student Branch and IIC at BGSIT.

---

## 🧠 Theme
**Build systems for rapid response. Enhance emergency services with smart software.**

---

## 📌 Objective
Participants are expected to develop innovative solutions that improve disaster management, emergency response, healthcare systems, or public safety using software technologies.

---

## 👥 Participation
- Teams from various colleges and districts are participating.
- Each team consists of multiple members.
- Only one member per team should upload the final code.
